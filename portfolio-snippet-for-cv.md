## n8n Portfolio Projects

**Lead Intake + WhatsApp + CRM Automation** — n8n workflow that captures form leads by Webhook, validates and normalizes data, scores lead priority, stores records in Google Sheets/PostgreSQL and triggers WhatsApp/email follow-up. Designed for commercial operations requiring fast response, traceability and low manual workload.

**Operational Alerts + Incident Escalation Automation** — n8n workflow that receives incidents by Webhook or Schedule Trigger, classifies severity, logs events and sends WhatsApp/email alerts with SLA-oriented escalation logic. Designed for back-office, technical support and provider monitoring.
